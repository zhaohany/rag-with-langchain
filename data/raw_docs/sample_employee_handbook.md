# Employee IT Support Quick Guide

## Password Reset

If an employee cannot log in, they should click "Forgot Password" on the login page.
If self-service reset fails, contact IT support with employee ID and department.

## Account Unlock

After 5 failed login attempts, accounts are locked for 15 minutes.
For urgent access, IT support can unlock the account in the admin panel.

## Laptop Troubleshooting

When a laptop is slow, first restart the machine and close unused applications.
If issues continue, check available disk space and install pending updates.

## VPN Access

Remote workers must connect via company VPN before accessing internal tools.
If VPN fails to connect, verify MFA and internet stability before creating a ticket.

## Escalation

Escalate to the platform team when incidents impact more than one department
or when service downtime exceeds 30 minutes.
